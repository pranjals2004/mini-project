Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, the Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi, India. It is a 73-meter tall tower, built in 1193, and is one of the tallest brick minarets in the world.

Kerala Backwaters

The Kerala Backwaters are a network of interconnected canals, rivers, lakes, and inlets located along the Malabar Coast. This serene destination is famous for houseboat cruises and its picturesque landscapes.

Varanasi

Varanasi is one of the oldest cities in the world, located on the banks of the Ganges River in Uttar Pradesh. It is a sacred city for Hindus and a spiritual center of India.
